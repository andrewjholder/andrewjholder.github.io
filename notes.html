Exchange 1 of 2
Your Question

What are some quick changes that will provide the most benefit to the site?

Response

Based on the audit, here are the highest-impact quick wins ranked by effort vs. payoff:

1. Add a "Book a Free Call" button to the navigation bar

This is the single fastest conversion fix. Right now there's no persistent CTA anywhere on the site. Every page a visitor lands on should have an obvious next step visible without scrolling. Takes 5 minutes in Squarespace.

2. Collect and add 2–3 testimonials immediately

Reach out to any past or current client today and ask for a one-sentence quote. Even something simple like "NEXPHASE saved me 8 hours a month on bookkeeping — I wish I'd hired them sooner." — Sarah M., salon owner would dramatically change how new visitors perceive trust. Put them on the homepage and each service page.

3. Rewrite the homepage headline

Change "Take control of your business" to something that actually says what you do:

Current: "Take control of your business"

Better: "Stop Doing Everything Yourself. NEXPHASE handles your bookkeeping, admin, and IT — so you can focus on the business you actually started."

This takes 10 minutes and immediately improves clarity for every new visitor.

4. Fix the five page title tags

Your current titles have no target keywords — search engines don't know what to rank you for. Update them to the versions in the audit report. This is a 20-minute fix in Squarespace's SEO settings panel and will start improving organic visibility within weeks.

5. Remove the "Dream it · Build it · Grow it" marquee

Replace it with a simple row of three trust signals: "QuickBooks Pro Advisor Certified · 20+ Years Experience · US-Based Team · Start in 1 Business Day." Same space, dramatically more persuasive.

6. Add internal links from your blog posts to your service pages

You have 4+ blog posts that get zero conversion value because they don't link anywhere. Add a single sentence at the end of each post like: "Struggling to keep up with your books? See how NEXPHASE's bookkeeping packages work →" This takes about 15 minutes total and immediately turns your blog into a lead-generation tool.

7. Clarify that services are remote/US-based on the homepage

Right now it's buried in FAQ sections. One line near the top — "Remote services for small businesses across the US" — answers a question many visitors have before they even scroll.

Priority order if you can only do three things this week: testimonials, nav CTA button, homepage headline rewrite. Those three changes alone will meaningfully improve how much a new visitor trusts you and what they do next.
